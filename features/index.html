Platform Features

Built for Modern Sales Teams

Every feature designed to help you book more meetings, engage more prospects, and keep your CRM data pristine — all from one platform.

Scheduling

Meeting & Calendar Management

Create professional booking experiences that convert visitors into meetings. Customise every aspect of your scheduling flow — from event types and availability to team routing and confirmation pages.

  • Unlimited event types with custom durations
  • Round-robin, priority, and weighted team routing
  • Buffer times & meeting limits to prevent burnout
  • Auto-detect timezones for global scheduling
  • Calendar sync with Google Calendar & Outlook
  • Embed booking widgets on any website
  • Auto-log meetings to HubSpot, Salesforce, or Clay
Engagement

Sales Engagement Campaigns

Build sophisticated multi-step email sequences with a visual builder. SyncLead's intelligent sending engine optimises delivery, prioritises engaged prospects, and respects provider rate limits — so your messages land in the inbox, not spam.

  • Step-based sequence builder with Add Step, variants & time delays
  • Auto-pause when a prospect replies
  • Open, click, and reply tracking with real-time analytics
  • A/B test subject lines and email copy
  • Optimised sending algorithms that prioritise engaged leads
  • Custom time-delays and send windows per timezone
  • Bi-directional CRM sync — every touchpoint logged
Multi-Account

Secure Account Bridging

Connect multiple Google and Microsoft email accounts securely via official OAuth APIs. SyncLead acts as a bridge — authenticating through Google's and Microsoft's own consent flows — never storing passwords or acting as an SMTP relay.

  • OAuth 2.0 authentication — no credentials stored
  • Connect unlimited Google & Microsoft 365 accounts
  • Send from any connected account within sequences
  • Unified view of all connected mailbox activity
  • Per-account sending limits & warm-up controls
  • Revoke access at any time from your provider settings

Inbox

Unified Master Inbox

Stop switching between email accounts. SyncLead's Master Inbox aggregates replies from every connected account into a single, searchable interface. See full conversation history, reply instantly, and sync every interaction to your CRM.

  • Aggregate replies from all connected accounts
  • Filter by campaign, account, reply status, or tag
  • In-line reply with templates & snippets
  • Full conversation history with contact timeline
  • Auto-sync to HubSpot, Salesforce, or Clay contacts
