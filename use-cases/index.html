Use Cases

Built for Teams That Close Deals

Whether you’re running outbound campaigns, managing client accounts, or nurturing inbound leads — SyncLead adapts to your workflow.

🎯

B2B Sales Teams

Combine outbound prospecting with seamless meeting booking. Create sequences that turn interested leads into booked meetings, and ensure every interaction is logged in your CRM.

  • Prospect via multi-step email sequences
  • Book meetings with one-click scheduling links
  • Auto-log every touchpoint to HubSpot or Salesforce
  • Track open, click, and reply rates in real-time
  • Enrich leads via Clay before outreach
🏢

Agencies

Manage multiple client accounts from a single dashboard. Connect client email accounts, run parallel campaigns, and report on performance — all without switching tools.

  • Multi-account management in one platform
  • Client-specific campaigns and sequences
  • Unified inbox for all client communications
  • CRM sync per client (HubSpot, Salesforce, Clay)
  • Per-account sending limits and compliance controls
💼

Consultancies

Capture inbound interest through intelligent booking links and automatically nurture leads that don't convert immediately. Keep your pipeline warm without manual effort.

  • Inbound scheduling with smart availability rules
  • Auto-nurture no-shows with follow-up sequences
  • Personalised drip campaigns for prospects
  • Calendar & CRM sync for complete visibility
  • Reply detection pauses sequences automatically
Outbound Sales

Build Pipeline on Autopilot

SyncLead is purpose-built for outbound sales teams that need to scale engagement without sacrificing personalisation. Import leads from Clay, enrich them with data, and launch personalised sequences — all while keeping your CRM up to date.

  • Import enriched leads from Clay or CSV
  • Multi-step sequences with conditional branching
  • A/B test messaging for higher reply rates
  • Auto-pause when a prospect replies
  • Activity logged to HubSpot or Salesforce deals
Inbound Scheduling

Convert Website Visitors to Booked Meetings

Embed SyncLead booking widgets on your website, landing pages, or emails. Prospects book meetings based on your real-time availability, while SyncLead creates contacts and deals in your CRM automatically.

  • Embeddable booking widgets for any webpage
  • Custom questions to pre-qualify leads
  • Team routing based on lead criteria
  • Confirmation & reminder emails to reduce no-shows
  • Auto-create contacts in HubSpot, Salesforce, or Clay
