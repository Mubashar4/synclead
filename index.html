Connect

Core Features

Everything You Need to Convert Leads Faster

From intelligent scheduling to automated follow-ups, SyncLead gives your team the tools to engage prospects at every stage of the pipeline.

Smart Scheduling

Manage your team's availability, route meetings intelligently, and generate customisable booking links that convert. Seamlessly embed scheduling into your workflow and CRM.

  • Custom availability rules & buffer times
  • Round-robin & priority-based team routing
  • Auto-detect timezones for global prospects
  • Sync meetings to HubSpot, Salesforce, or Clay

Automated Engagement Sequences

Build multi-step email campaigns with custom time-delays, A/B testing, and conditional branches. SyncLead's optimised sending algorithms prioritise engaged leads and respect provider sending limits.

  • Step-based sequence builder with variants & time delays
  • Auto-pause on reply for natural conversations
  • Open, click, & reply tracking with analytics
  • CRM activity logging — every touchpoint tracked

Unified Master Inbox

Manage all lead replies across multiple connected accounts in one single inbox. Never miss a critical response. Tag, prioritise, and reply directly — while every interaction syncs back to your CRM.

  • Single pane of glass for all email accounts
  • Smart filters: by campaign, status, or account
  • Reply templates & keyboard shortcuts
  • Bi-directional CRM sync with contact timeline
